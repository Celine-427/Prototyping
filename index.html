ZODIAC: Know Your Nature

A new era of harmony, identity, and order. Discover your assigned beast and help us preserve the memory of Earth's wildlife.

Serve Your Rank. Preserve the World.

ZODIAC brings people together. Each person gets an animal sign that shows their place and honors the animals we lost. Our world is hurt, so this sign is more than decoration—it is your duty. From the strong Tiger to the small Rat, every sign reminds us of Earth's old wildlife. Join us to keep balance, protect our technology, and save what is left.

Three Pillars of Society

High Tier — Tiger

Tigers stand for strong power and top leadership. They sit on the highest council, keep peace between sky‑cities, approve big building projects, and protect ZODIAC rules. Their bright cuffs, filled with tiny gems, send their name and rank across every network, showing everyone that the rulers are watching and guiding the future.

Mid Tier — Monkey

Monkeys are smart planners who adjust fast. They manage the tech and offices, turning council ideas into quick systems that keep big cities working well.


Low Tier — Mouse

Mice are quiet but important. They fix things on the ground and handle daily jobs, solving small problems so the big city keeps running.

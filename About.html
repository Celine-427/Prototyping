About ZODIAC

The ZODIAC Directorate is more than a classification system—it is our society's way to preserve the memory of Earth's vanished wildlife and maintain order in a fragile world. Each citizen receives a beast sign based on their personality, genetics, and social rank. From the noble Tiger at the top tier to the humble Mouse at the base, every animal teaches us respect, duty, and balance.

Our mission:

  • Honor Extinct Species — Keep alive the legacy of animals we once lost.
  • Maintain Social Harmony — Assign roles and responsibilities clearly.
  • Empower Every Citizen — From high leaders to ground workers, each sign has meaning.
